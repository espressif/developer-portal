## Meet Espressif at RustWeek 2026!

Espressif will be attending [RustWeek 2026](https://2026.rustweek.org/) in Utrecht, the Netherlands. The conference brings together over 900 community members, professionals, and Rust maintainers.

The week features workshops, two days of conference talks across three parallel tracks, a hackathon, the Rust Project's second All-Hands, an unconference, and social activities.

- :calendar: __Dates:__ 18–23 May 2026
- :round_pushpin: __Location:__ Kinepolis, Utrecht, Netherlands

## What you will see

The headline this year is the **1.1 release of [esp-hal](https://github.com/esp-rs/esp-hal)**, our `no_std` Rust HAL for Espressif chips. Building on the 1.0 stable API, 1.1 brings further driver coverage, ergonomics improvements, and continued support for our latest SoCs. Come talk to the team about what landed, what's next on the roadmap, and how to get the most out of writing Rust on ESP32.

We'll also have a booth full of interactive demos and games to play with, all running on Espressif silicon. Drop by to:

- Try out demos and games running bare-metal Rust on ESP32 hardware
- See `esp-hal` 1.1 in action across our latest SoCs (ESP32-P4, ESP32-C5, ESP32-C61)
- Chat with the esp-rs maintainers about embedded Rust, drivers, and async on microcontrollers

More details on the specific demos and giveaways in the upcoming weeks.
