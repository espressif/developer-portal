The Espressif Developer Portal is an open-source project maintained on [GitHub](https://github.com/espressif/developer-portal).

You, dear community members, are very welcome to collaborate on the portal and contribute in any way. Among the [possible contribution scenarios](contribution-scenarios "Contribution scenarios"), the most popular one is likely contributing content, such as blog articles or workshops.

Before you start writing content, prepare your local environment:

- Decide how you want to contribute by choosing the [contribution workflow](contrib-workflow "Contribution workflow")
- For local preview of the website, [install Hugo](https://gohugo.io/installation/) and go through its [Getting started](https://gohugo.io/getting-started/) if required. Alternatively, previews are automatically built in a pull request.

[espressif / developer-portal]: https://github.com/espressif/developer-portal "Espressif Developer Portal"

To write and deploy content, follow these guides:

- [Create article scaffold](create-article-scaffold "Create article scaffold")
- [Write and format content](write-and-format-content "Write and format content")
- [Validate and publish content](validate-and-publish-content "Validate and publish content")
